Duo Zhou

Hi, I'm a Ph.D. student at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign (UIUC). My research interests are in AI Safety, focusing on the theory and application of the following areas:

  • Trustworthy Machine Learning: I work on advancing machine learning robustness by addressing challenges like non-i.i.d. data and distribution shifts. My research also explores adaptable learning paradigms (e.g., federated, transfer, and continual learning) and adversarial robustness, including neural network verification and attack & defense strategies.
  • Sequential Decision Making Under Uncertainty: I study robust and distributionally robust approaches to reinforcement learning, online learning, and optimal control. My goal is to develop methods that ensure reliable and optimal performance in uncertain and dynamic environments.
